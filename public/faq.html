Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a cleaning service?

You can book a service directly through our website by clicking the "Book Now" button...

What’s included in a Standard Cleaning?

Standard Cleaning covers high-traffic areas and common surfaces to help maintain your home. It’s ideal for regular upkeep and is our most affordable option.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It’s up to you! Many customers provide access instructions so they don’t need to be present. All of our cleaners are background-checked and trusted.

What is the difference between Deep Cleaning and Move-out Cleaning?

Deep Cleaning focuses on deeply embedded grime and overlooked areas like behind furniture. Move-out Cleaning is for empty homes, aiming to restore the space to a move-in ready condition for inspections.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes! You can cancel or reschedule anytime from your account dashboard. Just make sure to do so at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a fee.
